Date Formatting

Today is
  • ii padded Minutes
  • i minutes
  • HH 24 Hours, padded
  • H 24 Hours
  • hh 12 Hours, padded
  • h 12 Hours
  • ss padded seconds
  • s seconds
  • dd Padded numeric day of the month
  • d Numeric Day of the month
  • DD Full day name
  • D Partial day name (first 3 letters)
  • mm padded numeric month
  • m numeric month
  • M Partial month name (first 3 letters)
  • MM Full month name
  • Y Full 4-digit year
  • y 2-digit year
  • a am/pm
  • A AM/PM
  • S Numeric suffix, i.e. 1st, 2nd, 3rd, ... 28th, 29th
  • {...}All text within brackets will remain unformatted
